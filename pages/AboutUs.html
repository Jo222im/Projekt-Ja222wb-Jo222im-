About the company

Welcome to a brand new thinking

We that work on this awesome place are two young and smart people. We have no limits!

OneHabbit began in december 2012 as a research project by Jens Ax and Jesper Obrelius, students at Linneus university who were working on web development. Their goal was “to develop the enabling technologies for a single, integrated and universal digital website during good graphics. Their business idea was to sell the stylish and casual art. In search of a dissertation theme, Page considered—among other things—exploring the mathematical properties of the World Wide Web, understanding its link structure as a huge graph. Their supervisor, Morgan Rydbrink, encouraged them to pick this idea (which Page later recalled as "the best advice I ever got")
